Dastan Aitkulov worked in engineering, business, and project management roles at Google for 11 years,
including work on such products and programs as Search, Gmail, YouTube, and Chrome.
Last 4 years he spent at Google X as a program manager focused on stratospheric balloons that
provide Internet in remote areas in emerging markets as well as a lead of a machine learning business.

Now he is a co-founder and CEO of Outtalent -
a career accelerator that helps engineers in emerging markets set and achieve ambitious goals.
